About me

CliftonStrengths: Connectedness, Restorative, Arranger, Context, Learner

I'm an life long learner trained across diverse cultures and disciplines. Always reaching further and pushing the boundaries of knowledge in different aspects. I love asking how stuff happens, and follow it with why. This curiosity led me into the STEM fields which I have greatly enjoyed. I am a proponent of utilizing any biotechnology available (as tools) with well though out stewardship plans to compact food insecurity across the world and mitigate the impacts of climate change. I also have a soft spot for small holder farmers. Did you know upto 80% of food consumed world wide is produced by small holder farmers?

My current job involves working across different disciplines within Bayer Crop Science Research & Development to ensure safe and responsible handling of biotechnology products across the globe. I very enjoy the dynamism, hands-on technical skills, and working with different teams to achieve the set business goals. At Bayer our Purpose is #ScienceForAbetterLife and our mission #HealthForAll #HungerForNone
